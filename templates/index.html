Welcome to my personal website!
Here you will find aspects about me ranging from my personal life to my professional life. I am a dedicated individual that is never bored - resulting in me exploring a range of hobbies to fill the void of time. I am an avid reader that enjoys a classic sci-fi trilogy, with the Red Rising series being my current favorite (although I am a big fan of Jonathon Scalzi's Old Man's War series). The nature of science fiction has guided my interest of science, where sometimes the inventions in a book become reality. The "pocket computer" from Jerry Pournelle's King David's Spaceship being one of them. Although I am not looking to invent the next pocket computer, I find myself wanting to contribute to science related to the green energy industry. My Ph.D. in chemical engineering at the University of Washington allowed me to be in an environment where I could tamper with expensive equipment to research the fabric of our universe. Fancy words for saying: I tried and failed a lot. I did, however, discovered some interesting catalytic abilites of nickel and nickel-based catalysts for the removal of urea from alkaline environments (found under publications). Post graduate school, I am currently seeking opportunites of employment in the greater Seattle area where I can apply my education and experience.

To making the world a more efficient place, one Joule at a time.
