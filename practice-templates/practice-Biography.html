Biography
I am a chemical engineer that is passionate to sovling the world's energy crisis through renewable energy means. During my academic career I have researched and developed thermo-chemical energy storage, biooil and biofuel synthesis, and organic molecule electrooxidation methods to help provide alternate technologies for a green economy.
During my research roles, I have designed, built, and further developed several apparatusses to characterize catalysts for the production of clean energy. High vacuum technology is an integral component in several of my devices, as it is necessary for several development and characterization methods, such as nickel single-crystal manufacturing and differential electrochemical mass spectrometry measurements, respectively.
About Me
